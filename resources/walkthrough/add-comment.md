## Adding Review Comments

You can add review comments in three ways:

1. **Hover** — Hover over a line and click **💬 Add Review Comment**
2. **Right-click** — Right-click in the editor → **AI Changes Review: Add Review Comment**
3. **Keyboard shortcut** — Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (`Cmd+Shift+R` on Mac)

Type your comment and press Enter. Comments are saved to `.vscode/.ai-review.json` and tracked automatically as you edit code.
